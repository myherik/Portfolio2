✨How to play✨

To move around press either the arrowkeys shown here

or use the "wasd" keys shown here

Basic instructions

Change colour of your snake by pressing 'c'

You can zoom in and out by pressing '+' or '-' respectively

Grow bigger by eating the red or green apples

Do not hit the border as you will die

Do not hit another snake as you will die

General information

Bigger snake = bigger score

Dead snakes turn into food equivalent to their score

Green apples = dead snakes
